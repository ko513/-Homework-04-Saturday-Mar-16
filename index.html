Indiana Jones and the Crystal Challenge

This adventure is easier and much less dangerous!

  • At the start of each game a (random) target score is displayed
  • The goal is to score exactly that many points by clicking on the crystals
  • Clicking a crystal adds points to your total
  • Each of the four crystals has a different points value, which you must deduce
  • You win the game if you "click in" the exact number of points shown in the Total box
  • You lose the game if you click in too many points. Overall won/loss totals are displayed
